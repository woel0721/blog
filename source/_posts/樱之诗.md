Question 1

**Context:** $U$ is a discrete random variable on $\{0, 1, 2, 3\}$. We are given $F_{U}(0.5)=0.25$, $F_{U}(2.5)=0.75$, and $P(U=3)=2P(U=1)$. $V$ takes values in $\{1, 2, 3, 4, 5\}$ with $F_{V}(y)=\beta y^2$.

### (a)

CDF and PMF

- **For $V$:** Since $F_V(5) = 1$, we have $\beta(5^2) = 1$, so **$\beta = 0.04$**.
    
    - $f_V(1) = F_V(1) = 0.04$.
        
    - $f_V(2) = F_V(2) - F_V(1) = 0.16 - 0.04 = 0.12$.
        
    - $f_V(3) = 0.36 - 0.16 = 0.20$.
        
    - $f_V(4) = 0.64 - 0.36 = 0.28$.
        
    - $f_V(5) = 1.00 - 0.64 = 0.36$.
        
- **For $U$:**
    
    - $P(U=0) = F_U(0.5) = 0.25$.
        
    - $P(U=3) = 1 - F_U(2.5) = 1 - 0.75 = 0.25$.
        
    - Since $P(U=3) = 2P(U=1)$, then $P(U=1) = 0.25 / 2 = 0.125$.
        
    - $P(U=2) = 1 - (0.25 + 0.125 + 0.25) = 0.375$.
        

### (b)

Joint Probabilities

- Given $P(V=4 \cap U \ge 2) = 0.12$.
    
- **$P(V=4 \cap U \le 1)$:** $P(V=4) - P(V=4 \cap U \ge 2) = 0.28 - 0.12 = \mathbf{0.16}$.
    
- **$P(V \ne 4 \cap U \ge 2)$:** $P(U \ge 2) - P(V=4 \cap U \ge 2) = (0.375 + 0.25) - 0.12 = \mathbf{0.505}$.
    

### (c)

Independence

- **No**, $U$ and $V$ are not independent.
    
- **Justification:** If independent, $P(V=4 \cap U \ge 2) = P(V=4)P(U \ge 2)$.
    
- $0.28 \times 0.625 = 0.175$, which does not equal the given $0.12$.

Question 2

**Context:** A biased spinner with $P(X=k) = c(2k+1)$ for $k \in \{1, \dots, 6\}$.

### (a)

Normalizing Constant

- $\sum_{k=1}^6 c(2k+1) = c(3+5+7+9+11+13) = 48c = 1 \Rightarrow \mathbf{c = 1/48}$.
    
- $P(X=4) = 9/48 = \mathbf{3/16}$.
    
- $P(X \ge 5) = P(5) + P(6) = (11+13)/48 = \mathbf{1/2}$.
    

### (b)

Expectation and Variance

- $E[X] = \frac{1}{48} \sum k(2k+1) = \frac{203}{48} \approx \mathbf{4.229}$.
    
- $E[X^2] = \frac{1}{48} \sum k^2(2k+1) = \frac{973}{48} \approx 20.271$.
    
- $Var(X) = E[X^2] - (E[X])^2 \approx \mathbf{2.386}$.
    

### (c)

Conditional Probability

- $P(X=5 | X \text{ is odd}) = \frac{P(X=5)}{P(1)+P(3)+P(5)} = \frac{11/48}{(3+7+11)/48} = \mathbf{11/21}$.
    

### (d)

Distribution of Y

- $Y$ follows a **Geometric Distribution**.
    
- Success probability $p = P(X=6) = 13/48$.
    
- $E[Y] = 1/p = \mathbf{48/13}$.
    
- $Var(Y) = \frac{1-p}{p^2} = \frac{35/48}{(13/48)^2} = \mathbf{1680/169}$.

## Question 1 / 第一题

**题目背景：** 离散随机变量 U 取值 {0,1,2,3}，其 CDF 满足 FU​(0.5)=0.25，FU​(2.5)=0.75，且 P(U=3)=2P(U=1) 。变量 V 取值 {1,2,3,4,5}，其 CDF 为 FV​(y)=βy2 。

+1

### (a) CDF and PMF / 累积分布函数与概率质量函数

- **For V:** 由于 V 在 y=5 时的累积概率必须为 1，即 FV​(5)=β(5)2=1，解得 β=1/25=0.04 。
    
    - **PMF fV​(v):** 通过 fV​(v)=FV​(v)−FV​(v−1) 计算：
        
        - fV​(1)=0.04(12)=0.04
            
        - fV​(2)=0.04(22−12)=0.12
            
        - fV​(3)=0.04(32−22)=0.20
            
        - fV​(4)=0.04(42−32)=0.28
            
        - fV​(5)=0.04(52−42)=0.36
            
- **For U:** * P(U=0)=FU​(0.5)=0.25 。
    
    - P(U≤2)=FU​(2.5)=0.75 。由此可知 P(U=3)=1−0.75=0.25。
        
    - 已知 P(U=3)=2P(U=1)，所以 P(U=1)=0.25/2=0.125 。
        
    - 最后 P(U=2)=1−P(0)−P(1)−P(3)=1−0.25−0.125−0.25=0.375。
        

### (b)

Joint Probabilities / 联合概率

- 已知 P(V=4∩U≥2)=0.12。
    
- **Compute P(V=4∩U≤1):** 由于 P(V=4)=P(V=4∩U≥2)+P(V=4∩U≤1)， 0.28=0.12+P(V=4∩U≤1)⇒0.16。
    
- **Compute P(V=4∩U≥2):** P(U≥2)=P(U=2)+P(U=3)=0.375+0.25=0.625。 P(V=4∩U≥2)=P(U≥2)−P(V=4∩U≥2)=0.625−0.12=0.505。
    

### (c)

Independence / 独立性判断

- **No, they are not independent.** (不独立)。
    
- **Reason:** 如果独立，则需满足 P(V=4∩U≥2)=P(V=4)×P(U≥2)。 计算：0.28×0.625=0.175。 但这不等于给定的 0.12。因此 U 和 V 不独立。

## Question 3 / 第三题

Part (a): Alice and Bob

- (i) P(at least one)=1−P(none)=1−(1−pA​)(1−pB​)=pA​+pB​−pA​pB​ 。
    
- (ii) P(exactly one)=pA​(1−pB​)+pB​(1−pA​) 。 P(both)=pA​pB​ 。
    

Part (b): Bernoulli Transformations / 伯努利变换

- U=max(X,Y)，V=min(X,Y)。
    
- (i) **PMF of U:** P(U=0)=(1−p1​)(1−p2​)；P(U=1)=1−(1−p1​)(1−p2​) 。 **PMF of V:** P(V=1)=p1​p2​；P(V=0)=1−p1​p2​ 。
    
- (ii) **Joint PMF:**
    
    - P(U=0,V=1)=0 (Max 不可能小于 Min)。
        
    - P(U=1,V=1)=P(X=1,Y=1)=p1​p2​。
        
    - P(U=0,V=0)=P(X=0,Y=0)=(1−p1​)(1−p2​)。
        
    - P(U=1,V=0)=P(X=1,Y=0)+P(X=0,Y=1)=p1​(1−p2​)+p2​(1−p1​)。
        
        **Independence:** U and V are **not** independent because P(U=0,V=1)=0=P(U=0)P(V=1) 。
        
- (iii) E[UV]=1⋅P(U=1,V=1)=p1​p2​ 。 Cov(U,V)=E[UV]−E[U]E[V]=p1​p2​−[1−(1−p1​)(1−p2​)](p1​p2​) 。
