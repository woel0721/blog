**Introduction (30-40 words)** 
"Hello everyone. Today, I want to talk about my goal for this semester: improving my English speaking skills. It is important to me because I want to use English more naturally and get a higher score in the EOS speaking test."

**The Plan (70-80 words)**
"My specific goal is to use at least three newly learned words from my daily vocabulary list in real conversations or during my EOS speaking tests.

I will measure my progress by using two main methods. First, I will check off the used words in my vocabulary app to see my growth. Second, I will record a two-minute video of myself speaking every day. This helps me check if my pronunciation is good or bed."

**The Action (70-80 words)** 
This goal is **achievable** because I have set a fixed time for it. "**I will** spend 15 minutes every morning learning 10 to 15 new words. Then, **I will** practice speaking them for 30 minutes to ensure I can say them clearly. Furthermore, **I will** focus on using these words actively in sentences. This is **realistic** because I am not just memorizing; I am making these words part of my daily communication."

**Conclusion (20-30 words)** 
"I will stick to this routine for 15 weeks and review my improvement at the end of the semester. I believe I can achieve this goal if I stay consistent.Thank you for listening!"
